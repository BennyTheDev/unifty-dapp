What is $DESK?

$DESK is a new loyalty token created by CoinDesk. Use it to maximize your experience at this year’s event by participating to earn rewards.

What can I do with $DESK?

You can use your $DESK on the NFT Marketplace to redeem for novel experiences, 1-on-1 meetings with VIPs, swag from our sponsors, or limited edition trading cards from our unique collections.

Where do I hold $DESK?

$DESK is held in your Torus wallet, though you can migrate to Metamask if you wish. Your Torus wallet can be accessed here, and via the icon in the bottom right corner of every page once you’re logged in.

Do I hold my keys?

Yes, Torus is a non-custodial wallet, so you and only you hold access to your $DESK once it’s been claimed.
