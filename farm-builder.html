Shop Addon Step 1 / 3

Step 1 / 3

By purchasing the shop addon for your farm, you will be able to transform your farm into a small shop, selling your items right away.

You will also be able to keep farming and provide a buyout option for each item from the farm.

Developer fee*:

*No developer fee for wildcard owners

Shop Addon Step 2 / 3

Step 2 / 3

You now need to allow the shop addon to manage NFT withdraws in behalf of the farm.

Shop Addon Step 3 / 3

Step 3 / 3

You now need to allow the shop addon to pause staking in behalf of the farm (in case the farm will be set to act as shop, only).

Edit Farm Shop

Here you can define in what run-mode your farm shop should operate

Shop Disabled = Farm operates in standard mode.

Farming + Buyouts = Farm operates in standard mode but allows buyouts for items that have a defined buyout price.

Only Buys = Staking disabled, only buying allowed. If you turn this on after people started staking, they will be able to continue staking and redeeming until they unstake their entire stakes.
